About PhishBlock

PhishBlock helps users identify suspicious emails, SMS messages and phishing links that may be used to steal money or personal information.

Common Warning Signs
  • Messages asking for passwords or PINs
  • Unknown phone numbers or email addresses
  • Links that look suspicious
  • Urgent requests for money transfers
  • Messages pretending to be from banks
  • Spelling mistakes and unusual language

Stay Safe Online

Never share your passwords, PINs or banking details with anyone. Always verify suspicious messages before taking action.
